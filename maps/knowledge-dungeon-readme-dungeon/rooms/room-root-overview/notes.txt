Summary
Knowledge Dungeon is a local-first, offline-friendly study dungeon-crawler that combines a room-based mindmap with structured note-taking and review.

Key Points
- It merges the repo-dungeon engine with mindmap-dungeon's learning model.
- Learners clear rooms by writing notes that pass deterministic quality gates.
- Clearing all rooms unlocks Archaeologist review prompts and streak tracking.

Recall Question
