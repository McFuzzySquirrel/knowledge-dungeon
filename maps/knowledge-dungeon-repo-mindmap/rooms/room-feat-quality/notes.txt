# Quality Scoring

## What Is Quality Scoring?

Quality scoring is the **numerical feedback** from validation that tells you how well you've explained a topic. It's the bridge between "did your note pass?" and "how good was it?".

## The Quality Bonus

After validation passes, a quality bonus (0–10) is calculated:

```
qualityBonus = (sum of all criterion scores) / 5 * 10
```

Example:
- All criteria score 2: bonus = 10/5 * 10 = 20 (capped at 10)
- Mixed scores (2+1+2+1+2): bonus = 8/5 * 10 = 16 (capped at 10)
- Minimal scores (1+0+1+0+1): bonus = 3/5 * 10 = 6

This bonus is **visible in the artifact** and feeds into **XP calculation** during progression.

## Why Quality Matters

The app assumes:

> **Better explanations = Better learning**

A student who writes clear, well-structured, interconnected notes is learning more deeply than one who submits barely-passing notes. The quality bonus incentivizes this.

### Progression Feedback Loop

```
High-quality notes → High quality bonus
                  → More XP awarded
                  → Rank up faster
                  → Badges/achievements
                  → Motivation to maintain quality
```

This is **virtuous cycle**: quality → progress → motivation → more quality.

## Preventing Inflation

Quality scoring is:

- **Deterministic** — Same note always gets the same score
- **Transparent** — You see exact scores for each criterion
- **Not gamifiable** — Can't artificially inflate by gaming the system
- **Aligned with learning** — Criteria reflect good study habits

## Displaying Quality

Quality is shown in several places:

1. **Encounter feedback** — After you submit
2. **Artifact document** — "Quality Bonus: 8/10"
3. **HUD** — Historical quality over time (optional)
4. **Progress review** — "Your average quality: 7.2/10"

## Design Insight

Quality scoring removes **arbitrary pass/fail** in favor of **continuous feedback**. You always know how well you did, not just "pass" or "fail". This motivates improvement and makes learning feel achievable and measurable.
