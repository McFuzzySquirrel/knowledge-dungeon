# Artifact Generation

## What Is an Artifact?

An artifact is a **markdown file** that captures your learning at a moment in time. It's part record, part portfolio, part study material.

## Artifact Contents

```markdown
# Scribe Artifact

- Artifact ID: artifact-{roomId}
- Subject: {subject name}
- Topic: {room topic}
- Room ID: {roomId}
- Generated At: 2025-01-15T10:30:00Z
- Quality Bonus: 8/10

## Rubric Breakdown
- sectionCompleteness: 2/2
- conceptTermCoverage: 1/2
- linkReferences: 2/2
- recallQuestionQuality: 1/2
- clarityReadability: 2/2

## Submitted Notes

{Your note text here}
```

## Where Artifacts Live

### Web (localStorage)
- Stored as blob in localStorage under key `artifact-{roomId}`
- Limited by storage quota

### Electron
- Saved to `dungeon-data/{subjectId}/rooms/{roomId}/artifact.md`
- Persists alongside notes
- Included in export/backup

## Why Artifacts Matter

### 1. Learning Record
- Proof that you cleared the room
- Snapshot of your understanding at that time
- Traceable over multiple subjects

### 2. Review Material
- During Archaeologist phase, you re-read your artifacts
- They jog your memory and trigger deeper thinking
- You can answer self-check prompts based on them

### 3. Portfolio Building
- Over a semester, you build a collection of artifacts
- Shows your learning journey
- Can be exported as a portfolio or study guide

### 4. Reflection Anchor
- Looking back at an artifact weeks later can show growth
- You see how your thinking has evolved
- Encourages metacognition

## Revalidation

If you later doubt your understanding:

- Open the artifact in Archaeologist phase
- Re-read your notes
- Trigger **revalidation** (costs nothing)
- Re-answer prompts with fresh perspective
- If convinced you learned it, mark reviewed

## Design Insight

Artifacts turn the ephemeral moment of clearing a room into a **permanent, revisitable learning record**. They're not just records—they're tools for spaced repetition and reflection. The artifact system treats learning as a journey with visible milestones.
