# Progression System

## What It Does

The progression system tracks **XP, ranks, and badges** as you defeat rooms and progress through phases. It's the leveling system that gives feedback and motivation.

## XP Calculation

When you defeat a room, you earn XP based on:

### Base XP
- Default: 100 XP per room

### Quality Bonus
- From the validation engine's quality score
- Ranges 0–10 points
- Example: quality score of 8 = 8 extra XP

### Streak Bonus
- Optional: bonus for completing rooms in rapid succession
- Max streak bonus: capped at ~20 points

### Total Earned
`totalXP = baseXP + qualityBonus + streakBonus`

## Ranks

XP thresholds unlock ranks in order:

- Novice (0 XP)
- Apprentice (~500 XP)
- Journeyman (~2000 XP)
- Expert (~5000 XP)
- Master (~10000 XP)
- Sage (unlimited)

## Badges

Special achievements unlock badges:

### Phase Badges
- **Creator Badge** — Unlocked when 90%+ of rooms are mapped (Creator-created rooms)
- **Scribe Badge** — Unlocked when all rooms are cleared (Scribe notes submitted)
- **Archaeologist Badge** — Unlocked after 2+ complete review passes (Archaeologist reviews)

## ProgressionSnapshot

Stored with each dungeon to track cumulative progress. Visible in the HUD during play.

## Design Insight

XP is **purely calculated**, not awarded manually. This ensures fairness and prevents progression exploits. Quality drives progression—better notes earn more XP.
