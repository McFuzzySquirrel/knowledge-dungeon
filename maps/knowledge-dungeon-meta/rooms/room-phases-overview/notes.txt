# The Three Phases

## Overview

Knowledge Dungeon structures learning in three **phases**, each with a different mode and goal. The phases are sequential—you unlock each one by completing the previous.

## Phase States

Every subject has a phase state:

- `SubjectCreated` — Brand new, no rooms yet
- `CreatorActive` — Actively building the topic map
- `CreatorComplete` — Map is finished (90%+ rooms designed)
- `ScribeActive` — Writing notes in rooms
- `ScribePartial` — Some rooms cleared, not all
- `ScribeComplete` — All rooms have passing notes
- `ArchaeologistUnlocked` — Eligible to review
- `ArchaeologistActive` — Reviewing artifacts and re-learning
- `SubjectMastered` — All phases complete, subject fully learned

## Phase Transitions

**Creator → Scribe**: Automatically, once you've created your first room

**Scribe → Archaeologist**: Automatically, once all rooms are cleared

**Archaeologist → Mastered**: Manually, after completing all reviews (or automatically with badges)

## Purpose of Each Phase

See the linked rooms for details, but in brief:

- **Creator** — Design the knowledge structure (play architect)
- **Scribe** — Fill in the knowledge (play explorer)
- **Archaeologist** — Consolidate the knowledge (play reviewer)

Together, they cover the **learning cycle**: Structure → Content → Review.
