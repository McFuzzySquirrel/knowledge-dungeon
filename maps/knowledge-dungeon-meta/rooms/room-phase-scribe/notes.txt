# Scribe Phase

## The Goal

In Scribe phase, you **fill in the knowledge** by walking through the dungeon and writing notes for each room. You're the scholar recording what you learn.

## What You Do

1. **Explore** — Walk into rooms using WASD or arrow keys
2. **Read the topic** — Understand what the room is about
3. **Encounter the challenge** — Press E to open the encounter modal
4. **Write notes** — Compose structured notes explaining the topic
5. **Submit** — Notes are validated automatically
6. **Defeat or retry** — Pass: collect artifact and XP. Fail: get feedback, revise, resubmit
7. **Repeat** — Clear all rooms to finish the phase

## The Note Structure

Your notes should include:

- **Overview** — What is this topic? Why does it matter?
- **Key Concepts** — The main ideas, terms, and definitions
- **Key Operations** — How do you use this? What can you do with it?
- **Design Insight** — Why is it designed this way? What's the philosophy?

(The validation engine looks for these sections.)

## Validation & Feedback

When you submit, the validation engine checks:

1. **All sections present?** — Must have all four sections
2. **Enough words?** — Minimum 100 words
3. **Key concepts covered?** — Mentions important terms
4. **References to other topics?** — Links to related ideas
5. **Clarity?** — No excessive jargon, reasonable sentence length

**You see the breakdown** of what passed/failed. Failed? Revise and resubmit (unlimited attempts).

## Progression

Each defeated room:
- Awards **base XP** (100) + quality bonus (0–10)
- Generates an **artifact** (your notes in markdown)
- Marks the room as **ArtifactCollected**
- Increases XP total (visible in HUD)

## Philosophy

Scribe phase is the **core learning loop**. Writing forces you to articulate understanding. Validation ensures quality. Artifacts create a record.

## Design Insight

Unlike passive reading, writing notes in Scribe phase **requires active thinking**. Validation prevents shallow submissions, ensuring you engage deeply with each topic. The artifact system means your Scribe notes become study material for future reviews.
