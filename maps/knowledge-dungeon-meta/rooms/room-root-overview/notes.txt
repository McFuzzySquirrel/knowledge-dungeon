# Overview

## What is This?

This mindmap explores the architecture, design, and implementation of Knowledge Dungeon—a study application that combines game mechanics with learning. You're literally learning *about* the game by playing *through* the game itself.

## The Big Picture

Knowledge Dungeon is a **local-first dungeon-crawler** where you:

1. **Create** a subject as a mindmap of topic-rooms (Creator phase)
2. **Explore** each room and write structured notes to "defeat" encounters (Scribe phase)  
3. **Review** your work and track learning streaks (Archaeologist phase)

## Key Insight

The app is built in layers:

- **Core domain** (graph, validation, progression) — pure domain logic, no dependencies
- **Game systems** (Phaser scenes) — rendering, navigation, encounters
- **UI & storage** (React, Zustand, electron bridge) — user interface and persistence

## How to Explore This Map

Follow the edges to discover:

- **Architecture & Core Domains** — the brains of the app
- **Technology Stack** — what we built it with  
- **The Three Phases** — the study loop
- **Game Systems** — how it renders and feels
- **Features & Mechanics** — what you actually interact with
- **Development Workflow** — how we maintain it

## Key Design Pattern

All learning happens via **structured notes with deterministic validation**. Every defeated room generates an artifact, tracks XP and ranks, and unlocks review opportunities in the final phase.
